Ellie E's Amazing Work

She/Her

▼ View Details

"Hello My Name is Ellie, I’m 18 years old and iv always felt out of place. Since I was little I always felt different to other children around me. I was quiet, I didn’t feel much happiness and after time learned to fake it like it was a natural instinct. I always followed and never lead as a person. Growing up when getting to teen hood I realised how much I didn’t relate to people around me which I struggled with. I also struggled with attraction. At first I thought it was wrong I liked how a girl looked and could image a relationship but some time later I learned about bisexuality which for the first time felt like me, felt like home, felt safe enough in a community. Telling my friends was the greatest decision. We have a friend group and when I told them they all were behind me accepting me for who I was and what I liked which gave me the confidence in future to be proud of being who I am. They all had my back, always being there for me and accepting me for my childish, bubbly and weird self which shaped the confidence I have today. Remember to never doubt yourself and to know you belong somewhere just about looking."

made with love, thank you all - Avery & Ashton
