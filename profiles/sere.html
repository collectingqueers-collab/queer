Love

By Sere

▼ View Poem

I like girls.
I think I've always known that, even if I never realised.
I like their smiles, their laughs, their tough softness.
I like them more than anything.

I also like boys.
Or maybe that's just what society says.
I think I may never know.
But I think I like them.

I like enbies.
I like how they're not afraid to be themselves
Or even when they are, how they do it anyway.
I will always like them.

I love people.
Anyone and everyone who lives or has lived.
I love their life, their light, their very being.
I love how they can always find a light in the dark.

I love love.
How it makes everything feel so warm and fuzzy and nice.
Platonic, romantic, familial, I love it all.
Love is a beautiful thing, regardless of the enactors.

The only thing I truly hate is society.
How it twists everything beautiful into something hard and painful.
How it worms its way into our minds and makes everything dark and black.
How when people try to be happy, it squashes their joy.

I hope one day society will be different.
Already there are people lighting up its gloom and cutting through its smog.
I love that hope.
It may not be enough yet, but it will be one day.
And I will love that day with all my heart.

made with love, thank you all - Polygraph & Ashton
