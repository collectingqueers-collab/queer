Why have we made this website?

We created this space because every queer young person deserves to see their own experiences reflected in art and stories. By providing a platform for our voices, we are building a community where we can celebrate our identities, support each other’s growth, and prove that our stories matter.

We hope to provide a plethora of stories for young queer people to see so they might see themselves in the work and not feel alone. Everyone's story is different, but every story might light a spark of hope in someone's life, and for that, we are grateful to be able to help.
