Why have we made this website?

We created this space because every queer young person deserves to see their own experiences reflected in art and stories. By providing a platform for our voices, we are building a community where we can celebrate our identities, support each otherâ€™s growth, and prove that our stories matter.

We hope to provide a plethora of stories for young queer people to see so they might see themselves in the work and not feel alone. Everyone's story is different, but every story might light a spark of hope in someone's life, and for that, we are grateful to be able to help.

How do I submit an entry/Contact you?

To submit an entry, you should contact us via email or Instagram (stated below). If you are going to submit an entry, we ask that you please follow this criteria:

  • You can submit any artwork, piece of literature, or even photography. We accept any form of expression; we just ask that you write a short paragraph about how it relates to your identity or story.
  • Please make sure not to add any personal details that you don't want to be public, such as addresses or private info.
  • Please state if you want to remain anonymous; we will happily accept a pen name to keep your privacy intact.
  • Last of all, we ask you to be respectful of everyone's identity. This is a place for everyone to feel welcome.
