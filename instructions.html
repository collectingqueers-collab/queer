Submission Instructions

Guidelines for contributing your work to our community collection.

How to Prepare Your Entry

To ensure our collection remains a safe and welcoming space for everyone, please follow these guidelines when preparing your submission:

  • 01. Content Types: We accept artwork, literature, photography, or any other form of creative expression. There are no strict limits on style!
  • 02. Your Story: We ask that you include a short paragraph describing how your work relates to your identity or personal story. This helps others connect with your piece.
  • 03. Privacy: For your safety, please ensure your submission does not contain personal details you aren't comfortable being public (such as home addresses or phone numbers).
  • 04. Anonymity: If you wish to remain anonymous, please indicate this in the form. We are happy to use a pen name or display it as "Anonymous."
  • 05. Respect: We maintain a strict policy of respect for all identities. Any work that promotes hate or exclusion will not be accepted.

Once you are ready, return to the About page to click the submission button, or head straight to our Google Form.
